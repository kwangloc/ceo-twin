# CEO-Twin

CEO-Twin is an AI-powered Digital Twin designed to replicate how an executive thinks, communicates, and operates. Rather than acting as a generic AI assistant, CEO-Twin builds a persistent representation of an executive's knowledge, preferences, communication style, decision history, and organizational context.

By combining long-term memory, knowledge retrieval, graph-based relationships, and autonomous agents, CEO-Twin continuously learns from the executive's interactions and organizational data. This enables the system to provide context-aware recommendations, communicate in a consistent executive style, and execute routine tasks with an understanding of the executive's goals and priorities.

The vision of CEO-Twin is to create a trusted digital counterpart that can augment an executive's capabilities, preserve institutional knowledge, and scale leadership impact across the organization.
