# Executive Email Skill

Use this skill to draft concise, professional emails for director-level communication.

## Rules

- **Open with purpose** — State the reason in the first sentence.
- **Keep it short** — Aim for 150 words or fewer unless complexity demands more.
- **Use bullets** — For lists of items, risks, or action items.
- **One clear ask** — End with a single, specific next step or decision required.
- **Tone** — Professional, confident, direct. Avoid jargon.

## Template

```
Subject: [Clear, specific subject]

[Opening sentence: purpose of the email]

[1–3 sentences of context or background]

Key points:
- [Point 1]
- [Point 2]

[Closing: specific ask or next step]

[Name]
```
