# Stakeholder Management Skill

Use this skill to map stakeholders, align expectations, and plan communication.

## Stakeholder Mapping

For each stakeholder, assess:
- **Influence** — Can they block or accelerate the work?
- **Interest** — Are they directly affected or passively involved?
- **Urgency** — Do they need immediate attention?
- **Ownership** — Are they accountable for a decision or deliverable?

## Communication Strategy

| Influence | Interest | Approach |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| High      | High     | Keep closely informed; involve in decisions |
| High      | Low      | Keep satisfied; brief updates only |
| Low       | High     | Keep informed; regular updates |
| Low       | Low      | Monitor; minimal communication |

## Output

- Stakeholder table (name, role, influence, interest, communication plan)
- Key risks from misaligned stakeholders
- Recommended next communication steps
